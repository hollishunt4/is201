California is the Steady Leader through the Years

The consistency in this state is unparallaled

Posted by Hollis Hunt on April 16, 2022

While the majority of U.S. states have an increase in profit, there are ten states that have negative profit. The states with the highest profit includes California ($76,381), New York ($74,039) and Washington ($33,403)

Profit in the State of California has increased at a steady rate from 2011 to 2014. Between those years profit has actually doubled going from about $250,000 in 2011 to about $500,000 in 2014.

Here the three main product categories, furniture, technology and office supplies, show that technology brings in more profit than the other two. Furniture is the least profitable but has still brought in $285,205.

Each year the quantity of sales takes a significant dip at the start of the year, mostly in the month of February. It is forecasted there will be a similar dip in the quantity of sales in the beginning of 2015. Sales towards the end of 2015 could reach record highs.
